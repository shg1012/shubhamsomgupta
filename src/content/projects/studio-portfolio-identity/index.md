## Design principles

- Let project imagery carry the voice.
- Use atmospheric depth without reducing readability.
- Keep navigation compact and always close at hand.

## Process

The system began as a visual exploration of contrast: rigorous interface grids against loose photographic and hand-drawn gestures.
