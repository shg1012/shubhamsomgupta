## Summary

The system gave product, marketing, and clinical communications a shared visual grammar while preserving room for context-specific tone.

## Design-system contribution

Colour roles, type hierarchy, illustration rules, and layout templates were translated into reusable design tokens and starter files.

## Reflection

The strongest identity systems are not just recognizable; they make future decisions easier for every team that touches them.
