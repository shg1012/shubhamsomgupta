## Opportunity

Operational leaders needed a faster way to see queue pressure, staff load, and intervention points before backlogs escalated.

## Process

The concept was built from task audits, alert taxonomy work, and lightweight prototype reviews with operational stakeholders.

## Outcomes or metrics

- Reduced dashboard scanning paths from five views to two primary views.
- Introduced risk tiers for overdue and blocked tasks.
