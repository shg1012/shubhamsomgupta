## Context

Technicians needed diagnostic support that could survive glare, interruption, gloves, and incomplete equipment history.

## Design principles

- Prioritise glanceable system status.
- Make the next physical action unmistakable.
- Allow documentation to happen in fragments.

## Final solution

The console combined fault timelines, guided checks, capture tools, and escalation notes in a tablet layout tuned for field use.
